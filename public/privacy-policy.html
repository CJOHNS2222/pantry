Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-23

Stock & Spoon ("we", "us", "our") provides a pantry management app that helps you track items, build shopping lists, and plan meals.

What we collect

We store minimal personal information in your household Firestore documents such as name, email, avatar and preferences. We may collect anonymous usage analytics to improve the app.

Camera permission

The app requests access to your device camera solely to scan package barcodes and take photos of pantry items. Images used by the app are uploaded to Firebase Storage only when you explicitly choose to save them; thumbnails and cached images are stored locally for faster loading.

How we use data

Data is used to provide app functionality (inventory, search, recommendations) and to improve the service. We do not sell your personal data.

Third-party services

We use Firebase services (Authentication, Firestore, Storage, Analytics). Please see their privacy documentation for details.

Contact

If you have questions, email: privacy@smartpantry.example

By using the app you consent to this policy.
