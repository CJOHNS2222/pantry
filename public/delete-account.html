Request Account Deletion — Smart Pantry

Last updated: 2026-02-23

This page explains how to request deletion of your Smart Pantry account and associated data. The developer name shown on the Play Store is Smart Pantry.

How to request deletion

  1. Sign in to the app and go to Settings → Privacy & Legal and follow the "Request account deletion" link; or
  2. Email us at chrisj221986@gmail.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request". Include the email address associated with your account and your full name. If available, include your user ID shown in the app.

What we will delete

When we process your request we will delete or anonymize the following data that is specific to your account:

  • Your user profile (name, avatar, preferences) stored in Firestore.
  • User-scoped cache entries (e.g., `users/{userId}/cache/*`).
  • Images you uploaded to Firebase Storage (pantry item photos) associated with your account, where feasible.
  • Authentication credentials for your account (Firebase Authentication user record).

What may be retained

Some data may be retained or partially retained for operational, legal, or safety reasons:

  • If you are a member of a household, inventory items owned or managed by the household may remain to preserve the household data for other members. Household owners may need to remove shared items or request household deletion separately.
  • Aggregated or anonymized analytics used for service improvement are not tied to your personal identifier and will be retained.
  • Logs or backups may be retained for a short retention period for security or compliance; these are removed or rotated according to our retention policies.

Retention period

We will process deletion requests within 30 days of verification, unless additional verification or legal holds are required. You will receive a confirmation email when the deletion is complete.

Questions

If you have questions about the deletion process, contact: chrisj221986@gmail.com
