Take a closer look at the actual tools and workflows inside Stock & Spoon.

Inventory Management

Smart Pantry Tracking

Our inventory screen lets you visually inspect container fill levels (1/4, 1/2, 3/4, Full) and auto-generates expiry alerts based on FDA food safety presets.

  • Visual quantity modifiers for quick inventory adjustments
  • FDA-aligned safety alerts and smart shelf-life defaults
  • Quick filtering by storage location (Freezer, Fridge, Spices)
Meal Planning

Drag-and-Drop Planner

Schedule meals for the entire week. The meal planner calculates exactly what ingredients you are missing and links shopping items directly to the recipe source.

  • Bypasses redundant modal menus with direct drag-to-slot scheduling
  • Lists recipe sources on your shopping items (e.g. "🍳 need 2 tbsp for Tacos")
  • Calculates nutrition details for scheduled days
AI Assistant

Recipe Generator & Search

Search or generate creative recipes using your available pantry items. Enter ingredients manually or let the app highlight matching recipes from the community.

  • Optimized local recipe caching on search tab re-mounts
  • Rate, customize, and save modifications to community recipes
  • Step-by-step cooking timers integrated directly into instructions
Grocery Shopping

Smart Shopping List Checkout

Add ingredients quickly using the 'Quick Add' suggestion tray. When checking out, the app automatically prompts for expiration dates using smart defaults based on the food type.

  • Consolidated select-all controls and clean swipe gestures
  • Expiration checkout prompts that float cleanly above navigation tabs
  • Preserves staples that reappear automatically when depleted
User Interface

Adaptive Styling

Whether you're cooking in a dim, cozy kitchen or standing in a brightly lit supermarket aisle, Stock & Spoon transitions seamlessly between light and dark themes to optimize readability.

We've eliminated hardcoded palettes (such as burgundy) and aligned our entire theme to match system-wide design tokens for a premium, unified user experience.
