Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 14, 2026

Welcome to Stock & Spoon ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and website.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We may collect the following personal information when you register or interact with our service:

  • Email address (for account creation, invites, and system communication)
  • Name and profile information (display name, dietary restrictions, height, weight, diet goals)
  • Device information and push notifications preferences
  • Usage data, crash reports, and system performance metrics

Pantry and Recipe Data

To provide core household coordination, we securely store:

  • Pantry stock quantities, visual level modifiers, and expiration dates
  • Weekly meal plans, recipe schedules, and custom recipe modifications
  • Active shopping lists, checkout histories, and grocery item aisle classifications
  • Household synchronization mappings (linking family members' accounts to a shared household database)

Automatically Collected Information

We automatically collect technical details to support offline usage and performance monitoring:

  • Device model, operating system version, and unique installation identifiers
  • Firestore read/write monitoring logs and offline queue status
  • Sentry crash metrics and app diagnostics logs

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To synchronize pantry stock, shopping lists, and meal plans between household members in real-time
  • To calculate smart safety alerts, USDA-aligned expiration presets, and AI-powered recipe search matches
  • To manage subscription tiers (free, premium, family) and verify in-app purchases securely
  • To log error events and monitor database cache performance (minimizing redundant read operations)
  • To communicate system updates or invite links sent at your request

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We respect your privacy and do not sell or lease your personal data. We only share information under the following criteria:

  • Household Members: Your name, dietary goals, list selections, and online status are shared with active members of your verified household.
  • Service Providers: Secure metadata is processed by Firebase (database/hosting), Sentry (crash tracking), and Stripe (payment processing) under strict data protection protocols.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect safety, security, and integrity of the service.

Data Security & Caching

We implement industry-standard security measures, including HTTPS encryption in transit and secure database rules in Firestore. Local caches (using localStorage) keep your inventory accessible offline and prevent redundant reads from your device.

Your Rights

You have full control over your data. At any time, you can request data correction, export, or choose to delete your account permanently (which deletes your user profile and personal collection documents instantly from our database).
